## French Ambassador's Visit to the ATCC Center

On July 8, 2022, the French Ambassador to Thailand, Mr. Thierry Mathou, visited the Anti-Human Trafficking and Child Abuse Center (ATCC) in Pattaya.

### The ATCC Center

The ATCC, operating under the FACE Foundation (Fight Against Child Exploitation) since late 2011, has been fighting human trafficking and child sexual abuse for over 15 years.

The center assists victims both socially and legally, providing safe shelter, education, and support for resocialization.

### France's Commitment

The Ambassador reaffirmed France's commitment to fighting child exploitation and emphasized operational cooperation with Thai authorities through expert training programs.
