## Donation to the Child Protection Center 2016

During the June 2016 Installation Night, the Rotary Club Pattaya Marina presented a donation of **18,000 baht** to Khun Toy, director of the Child Protection Center, affiliated with Human Help Network Foundation Thailand.

This donation was made possible through the generosity of members and participants at the annual installation event for the club's new board.
