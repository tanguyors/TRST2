## Donation to the Child Protection Center

On May 11, 2022, Heidi Glemeau, Vice President of the Rotary Club Pattaya Marina, donated **100,000 baht** to Khun Toy of the Child Protection and Development Centre (CPDC).

"It is but a drop in an ocean of misery, but drops of water eventually become a river."

### The CPDC

The CPDC is part of the Human Help Network Foundation Thailand and addresses the exploitation and poverty of street children through integrated protective services: field work, welcome centers, permanent residences, and prevention programs.

### Impact

- 100,000 baht donation for child protection
- Support for street children assistance programs
- Every cent donated is used for the project
