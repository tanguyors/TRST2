## Dormitories for Karen Children - Padae

The Rotary Club Pattaya Marina built new dormitories with bathrooms for girls and boys at the Padae center in Karen territory.

### The Project

This infrastructure project provided dignified living conditions for the children housed at the Padae center, a remote and hard-to-reach area.

### Impact

- Separate dormitories for girls and boys
- Modern bathrooms
- Significant improvement in living conditions
- Enhanced safety for the children
