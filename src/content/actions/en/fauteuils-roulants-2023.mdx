## Wheelchairs and Medical Mattresses

As part of a District 3340 initiative, the Rotary Club Pattaya Marina contributed **15,000 baht** to the purchase of wheelchairs and inflatable medical mattresses for people in need.

### Delivery

On January 11, 2023, the equipment was delivered to the Father Ray Foundation, a long-standing partner of the club in its social work in Pattaya.

### Impact

- 15,000 baht contribution for medical equipment purchases
- Delivery to the Father Ray Foundation
- Improved quality of life for people with disabilities
