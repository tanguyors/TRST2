## Clean Water for Karen Villages

The Rotary Club Pattaya Marina carried out several projects to install water distribution and filtration systems in Karen villages, located in remote areas of Thailand.

### Huei Kra Ting Village

Located 70 km north of Mae Sot, the village of Huei Kra Ting was equipped with a complete clean water distribution and filtration system.

### Kre Kee Village

On January 21, 2017, the club installed water pipes and filtration systems in the village of Kre Kee, located 150 km from Mae Sot.

### Mae Way Village

Water filters were provided to the village of Mae Way, situated along the Myanmar border.

### Mae Song Noi Village

In the Karen region, the village of Mae Song Noi also received water tanks and filters, as well as replacement batteries for storing electricity generated by solar panels.

### Lasting Impact

These projects have transformed the daily lives of hundreds of families by providing them with access to safe and reliable drinking water.
