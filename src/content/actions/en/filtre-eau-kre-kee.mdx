## Water Supply - Kre Kee

The Rotary Club Pattaya Marina implemented a drinking water system in Kre Kee village (150 km from Mae Sot) during 2016-2017.

### The Village

The village has 56 households and 297 residents, with 67 students (kindergarten through 6th grade) and 4 teachers. The population is 80% agricultural workers and 20% livestock farmers, 97% Buddhist and 3% Catholic.

### The System

The installation produces approximately **300 liters of water daily** through eight filtration centers using gravel, active sand, and charcoal filters. The system uses four 200-liter tanks with PVC piping connectors.
