## Human Help Network & Baan Jin Jai

The Rotary Club Pattaya Marina actively supports the Human Help Network Foundation and its child protection programs in Pattaya.

### Donation to the CPDC - May 2022

Heidi Glemeau, Vice-President of Rotary Pattaya Marina, donated **100,000 baht** to the Child Protection and Development Center (CPDC), managed by the Human Help Network.

### Installation Night 2016

During the Installation Night ceremony in June 2016, President Jo Klemm and President Michel Roche presented **18,000 baht** raised for the Human Help Network Foundation.

### Baan Jin Jai Orphanage

The club regularly provides essential supplies to the Baan Jin Jai orphanage in Nongprue, which shelters children in vulnerable situations.

### Petanque Tournament

On March 14, 2017, a triples petanque tournament was organized with one child from the orphanage on each team, followed by a sauerkraut dinner evening, in support of the association.
