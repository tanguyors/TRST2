## March 8, 2023 - Women's Day

On March 8, 2023, the Rotary Club Pattaya Marina, together with Rotary Sattahip and the Pattaya Provincial Lawyers Council, organized a solidarity operation at Pattaya Special Prison, Nong Pla Lai district, Chonburi province.

### Our Action

Over **40,000 baht** worth of essential items and hygiene products were distributed to female inmates. "Unity is strength" was the motto of this day, themed **EMPOWERING GIRLS**.

More than 200 inmates among the 2,000 incarcerated persons benefited from this distribution of essential goods.

### Impact

- Distribution of hygiene products and essential items worth over 40,000 baht
- More than 200 female inmates benefited
- Partnership with Rotary Sattahip and Provincial Lawyers Council
- Raising awareness about the conditions of incarcerated women
