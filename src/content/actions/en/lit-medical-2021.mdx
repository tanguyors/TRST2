## Medical Bed for Nong Health Center

On February 9, 2021, the Rotary Club Pattaya Marina delivered a medical bed to the Nong Public Health Center.

### Essential Equipment

This center serves approximately 20 patients daily, including children from the nearby orphanage. The medical equipment significantly improves care conditions for both patients and healthcare staff.
