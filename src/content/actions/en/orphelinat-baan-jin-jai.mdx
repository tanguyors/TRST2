## Baan Jing Jai Orphanage

The Rotary Club Pattaya Marina supports Baan Jing Jai orphanage located at Soi Sukhumvit 53, Pattaya, housing approximately 80 orphaned or abandoned children aged from a few months to 18 years.

### Timeline of Our Commitment

- **June 2012**: Club alerted to facility conditions
- **September 3, 2012**: Initial assessment visit
- **September 28, 2012**: Director invited to weekly club meeting
- **December 2012**: Delivery of washing machine and 3 clotheslines
- **February 2013**: Distribution of fruits, clothing, and hygiene items
- **June 2013**: Additional aid delivery
- **April 2014**: Barbecue event with clowns, musicians, and individual gifts

### Regular Support

Monthly aid of approximately **5,000 baht** covers essential needs for the children and the facility.
