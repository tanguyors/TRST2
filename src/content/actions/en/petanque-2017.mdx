## Petanque Tournament - March 14, 2017

On Tuesday, March 14, 2017, the Rotary Club Pattaya Marina and Pilouterie Petanque Khao Rai organized a petanque tournament in triples with one orphanage child per team, benefiting Human Help Network Foundation Thailand.

### The Event

Entry fee was 200 baht per person, with all proceeds going to the organization. 16 teams competed in the tournament.

Winners: Nali Alain Didier (3 victories, +27).

### Results

The funds raised, **25,000 baht**, were used to purchase washing machines and meet facility needs. The evening concluded with a choucroute dinner in a convivial atmosphere.
