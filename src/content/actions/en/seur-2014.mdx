## Heartt 2000 Foundation - 2014 Donation

On November 14, 2014, the Rotary Club Pattaya Marina presented Dr. Philippe Seur with a donation of **52,000 baht**.

### Vital Support

The doctor explained the financial difficulties encountered by the foundation. These funds were designated for purchasing antiretroviral drugs to treat AIDS patients without financial means.

Dr. Seur's Heartt 2000 foundation continues its essential work with HIV-positive patients in the Pattaya region.
