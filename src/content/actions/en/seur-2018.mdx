## Charity Evening - December 1, 2018

On December 1, 2018, the Rotary Club Pattaya Marina organized a charity evening at the Casa Pascal restaurant benefiting Dr. Philippe Seur's Heartt 2000 association.

### Ongoing Commitment

Dr. Seur received a donation of **70,000 baht** on December 7, 2018. Since arriving in Pattaya in 1998, Dr. Seur has assisted 2,785 patients. He currently treats approximately 600 HIV-positive patients, all receiving free care.
