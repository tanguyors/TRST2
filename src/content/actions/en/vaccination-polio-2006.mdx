## Polio Vaccination - December 13, 2006

On December 13, 2006, the first polio vaccination campaign was organized by Pattaya City Hall, the Ministry of Health, and Rotary International.

### The Action

The program targeted disadvantaged children and was offered free annually across Thai municipalities. Club members participated in administering the vaccines.

### Follow-up

A follow-up vaccination was scheduled for January 17, 2007. The initiative began with club members attending the annual Polio Plus conference at the Rotary Center in Bangkok on November 23, 2006.
