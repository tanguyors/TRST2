## World End Polio 2019 - Tribute to François Lecomte

On Thursday, October 24, 2019, the Rotary Club Pattaya Marina organized its annual fundraising evening at the Pullman G Pattaya Hotel, dedicated to the memory of François Lecomte, former club secretary who passed away in 2018, having contracted polio at the age of 8.

### Conference

Dr. Olivier presented the history and current challenges of polio. Governor Maruay Jintabanditwong and delegations from regional Rotary clubs were in attendance.

### Results

Approximately **45,000 baht** was collected, multiplied by the district (×2) and the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation (×3), totaling **270,000 baht** or approximately $9,000 USD.
