## World End Polio 2020

On Saturday, October 24, 2020, the Rotary Club Pattaya Marina organized a fundraising evening at the Royal Cliff Hotel in Pattaya under the theme "END POLIO NOW".

### Conference and Events

Dr. Olivier presented a conference on polio eradication, covering historical context from ancient Egypt to current challenges. PDG Peter Malhotra provided translation.

The evening included an art auction, a lottery with numerous prizes, and a buffet dinner.

### Results

- **89,670 baht** collected in total
- **70,000 baht** directed to District 3340 for World Polio Day initiatives (with matching and tripling through the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation)
- **19,670 baht** allocated to other club charitable activities
