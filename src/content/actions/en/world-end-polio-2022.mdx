## World End Polio 2022

On October 21, 2022, the Rotary Club Pattaya Marina organized its annual gala evening at the Siam Bayshore Resort in Pattaya, dedicated to global polio eradication.

### Commitment Despite the Crisis

Despite two and a half years marked by the coronavirus health crisis and the economic impact of the pandemic, the club still managed to raise more than **41,000 baht**.

### Results

A total of **42,000 baht** was transferred to the Rotary Foundation for polio eradication efforts through District 3340. The convivial evening included a buffet dinner bringing together club members and District Rotarians.
